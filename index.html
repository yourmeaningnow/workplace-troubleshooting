A lot of people are unhappy at work. However through reflection and analysis we can find what we like, and dislike, and build stepping stones towards better and better jobs. The perfect job isn't something you just land in. Through your own experience we can come up with the best job dynamics-formula for you, so you can get to work in some of the places that you care the most about, doing work that you are actually happy to do.

This analysis walks you through the five dynamics that make or break a job. And helps you to understand 1. how much each of these dynamics matter to you, and 2. how satisfied you are with how each of these dynamics play out in you work, right now.

Part two · How much each matters

Where do you place your weight?

Drag each toward how much it genuinely matters to you in a job — not how much you think it should. There are no wrong answers here.

Part three · Your job right now

How's it actually going?

Answer about the job you have now (or your most recent one). Go with your gut — the first reaction is usually the honest one.
